About The Climbing Frame Factory

Highlights

We are a local Co. Armagh Firm with over 30 years combined experience working in all things Wooden. Our climbing frames are designed for children aged from 3-14 years and are ideal for both girls and boys. Our wooden climbing frames look great, are made from quality products but more importantly provide hours of fun in your own garden.

Products

Our climbing frame range includes playsets which are specially designed for all size gardens and play spaces with play equipment which is ideally suited for younger and older children. We can include the most popular accessories, such as swings, slides, monkey bars and climbing rock walls and all our frames and garden play features can be designed for your children, budget and garden in mind.

Bespoke Products

We are able to make anything wooden to your requirements at a price that suits you from the highest quality products.
